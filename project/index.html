Welcome to Belém

Located in northern Brazil at the entrance of the Amazon River, Belém is known for its rich history, colonial architecture, vibrant markets, tropical cuisine, and beautiful waterfront. Visitors enjoy exploring museums, parks, historic churches, and experiencing the unique culture of the Brazilian Amazon.

Featured Attractions

Ver-o-Peso Market

One of the largest open-air markets in Latin America, famous for regional fruits, seafood, herbs, and Amazonian culture.

Estação das Docas

A restored riverfront warehouse district featuring restaurants, live music, shopping, and spectacular sunset views.

Mangal das Garças

A beautiful ecological park showcasing Amazon wildlife, gardens, butterflies, and observation towers.
